Getting things done from emergent design and unit testing. The concepts all fit together quite nicely, once you get into it.

categories : - April 2006 - "Mike Stenhouse" comments : | ---

Pascal mentioned it in his Unit testing tutorial post but emergent design has changed the way I see coding… I work with Marcus (lastcraft) and he’s had his work cut out for him converting me, but I’m now a born again test driven designer. I’ve also started to apply the same philosophy to loads of other things. I’m slowly moving over the the Getting things Done way of thinking and I let my designs evolve the same way. I’ve even started using a bulldog clip and index cards as a note pad…

CwS supports London’s first netlabel festival with more than 30 artists from all over the world. Netaudio’06 takesplace on the 15th and 16th of September 2006 at Candid Arts Gallery and Electrowerkz, next to Angel tube station, London.

Make sure to pop down and check out a fine selection of netlabel music,
audiovisual arts, take part in the knowledge fair and coffe table discussions.

The daytime event will be for free, tickets for both nighttime events
are now available for £15 through Ticketweb

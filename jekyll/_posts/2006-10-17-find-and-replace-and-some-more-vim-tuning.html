Awkward at first glance VIM has become my editor of choice. A modified .vimrc file based on the one found at schlitt.info
enables quite a couple of extras that make work fast and efficient.

Handy as well is the functionality to add comments, again, found at schlitt.info and available for download at vim.org

Neat: Search and Replace in VIM works by utilizing the sed stream editor.
